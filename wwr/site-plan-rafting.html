Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to contribute to the increment and development of family recreation while improving the financial growth of the business to guarantee its success in all areas mentioned and future expansion.

Audience

Our audience is composed largely of families, couples or individuals that are looking for good family entertainment and recreation. Mostly aimed at the middle and upper class. Our audience will look for a canoeing, kayaking or rafting and we will provide it at a very reasonable price. The location where we will provide our services doesn't have any other water parks and we will meet those needs. The audience will access the website via desktop, laptop, and mobile device
