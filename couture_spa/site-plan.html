Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to attract clients to our beautiful Spa and to provide them with an out-of-this-world experience, combining beauty, comfort, knowledge and laser focus attention to all your needs. Providing an elite, world class personal experience that will be both unforgetable and rejuvenating to all.

Audience

Our audience is composed largely of individuals (18+) that are looking for spa services that willl help them maintain a youthful aesthetic. We are looking to target specially professional, consistently regular clients that live in the Ft. Lauderdale area.

Content

Home page

This page will be used for our clients to be able to understand what we do but more for appealing to their want or need for the experience of top of the line rejuvanating care. It will introduce our Spa and entice the reader for an out-of-this-world experience like no other. The way the website is created has to convince the reader of that. By making the page very clear, enticing and displaying the benefits and results of a very sofisticated yet dedicated treatment, it will not only attract the correct clientele but spark curiosity if built correctly.

(Relax, close your eyes, take a deep breath and prepare for an unforgettable experience, with our finesse and dexterity as your guide and relaxation as your destination in our beautiful Spa in Fort Lauderdale. Scented candles, natural oils, the mist of the ocean and the whisper of relaxing melodies that can transpose your mind to a paradise are the enigma of the Couture Spa Experience. Our magical touch that will allow you to free and relax your mind and body while our massages and practice, created to dissipate the stress of everyday life, will bring you to a complete state of serenity. Awaken your senses in a unique experience and let us transport you to a Fountain of Youth where you will only feel calm, peace and tranquillity.)
